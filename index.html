What is Staking in ADA?

To take part in the Proof Of Stake process, users save up digital assets(ADA) to form a 'stake,' and delegate it to those who do all the upkeeping work and get a share of the reward in return. This process is called staking. By staking, you take part in the consensus mechanism, and the nodes that work as staking pools, share a part of their rewards, according to your stake with them.
